About Me


My story in pursuing a tech career began in March of 2020. As COVID-19 began spreading nationally, the governmental shut down left me without a job. During this time, I did a lot of self-reflecting. I knew I wasn't happy in my previous career and decided the best thing for me was to attempt a career change. I knew the only way for this to be possible, and even remotely successful, was to go back to school. Here I was, about to start all over. The feeling of apprehension and self-doubt lingered at the thought of such a drastic undertaking. However, I knew I was going to try my best at this very important transitional chapter of my life. I took the leap of faith.


During my journey in my Master's program, I became educated in Statistics, Business Intelligence through Analytics, Database Management, Optimization Methodologies, Data Visualization, Business Processes for Analytics, Data Mining and Big Data Management. Additionally, my Microsoft Excel skills improved drastically. But more importanly, I developed a passion for data and improving businesses through the help of analytics!


I earned a Master of Science in Business Analytics and I'm eager to break into the tech industry! I hope to be a part of a team that champions data-driven decision making to maximize revenue, limit expenses, or to simply understand characteristics of the business from an analytical perspective.

  3. Solution-oriented mentality
  4. Resourceful
